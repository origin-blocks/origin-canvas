# Style Variations

Origin includes style variations that let you change the visual direction of the site without changing the underlying templates.

## What Style Variations Change

Style variations can change colors, typography, and the overall visual tone of the theme.

They are a starting point. You can still adjust individual settings after choosing one.

## Choose a Variation

Go to Appearance > Editor > Styles.

Browse the available styles, choose the variation that best fits the site, then save the change when you are ready.

## Included Variations

Origin includes a base style plus color and typography variations.

Use color variations when you want a different palette. Use typography variations when you want a different type direction while keeping the same general structure.

## After Choosing a Variation

After selecting a variation, you can continue editing individual style settings in the Site Editor.

Those custom changes are saved for your site and may override the theme's original defaults.
